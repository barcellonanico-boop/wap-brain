## Strategic Reframe (Critical — read first)

The story is NOT "post is rotting." The story is:

1. **Position IMPROVED YoY** from 17.14 → 9.78. Google moved us UP a full page.
2. **CTR collapsed** from 3.63% to 1.01% over the same period.
3. **CTR is the problem, not rank.** Even though we rank better, fewer clicks per impression = title/snippet doesn't earn the click anymore.
4. **QoQ shows recovery** in motion. Clicks +27%, position +2.71, CTR +0.26pp. The slope is positive.
5. **What changed YoY to crush CTR?** Likely culprits:
   - Title says "...Stay 2025" — year-stamped to last year, kills freshness
   - SEO title vs H1 mismatch (Step 2 flagged)
   - AI Overviews appeared on multiple "best area" queries (cannibalization confirmed below)
   - Rich SERP features eating click distribution (PAA box at #3 on primary)
   - Reddit/TripAdvisor/NYT/Telegraph all in top 10 with DA 90+

**Rewrite priority:**
1. Don't break the QoQ recovery slope (slug stays, major H2 keywords stay)
2. Fix CTR via title + meta description rewrite (kill 2025 stamp, install Nico voice hook)
3. Get cited in AI Overviews (first-540-words structured answer + FAQPage schema + TL;DR table)
4. Voice differentiation = moat against DA 90+ competitors
