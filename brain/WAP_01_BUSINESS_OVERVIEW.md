# WAP_01_BUSINESS_OVERVIEW.md — Business Overview

Last updated: April 19, 2026

---

## 1. What Is wearepalermo.com

wearepalermo.com is an English-language online guide to Palermo, Sicily. It was born as a blog and remains primarily content-driven. The site helps English-speaking tourists — mainly Americans — experience Palermo like a local, avoiding tourist traps and getting honest, practical advice.

The business runs on autopilot. There is no team. Nico does everything.

---

## 2. The Face of the Brand: Nico Barcellona

Nico is a true Palermitan. He is not a travel influencer. He is an authentic storyteller who presents Palermo without filters, sugarcoating, or censorship.

His communication style: direct, sharp, no-nonsense. He talks to travelers like a friend at a bar — sarcastic, funny, packed with personal anecdotes, and always brutally honest. Think stand-up comedian meets local expert. His USP is that people trust him because he sounds real, not like a travel blog.

He is not present enough. The site runs largely on old content. The goal of the WAP Content Machine project is to fix this with AI.

---

## 3. Target Audience

Primary: American and English-speaking tourists planning a trip to Palermo or Sicily.

Secondary:
- European travelers looking for practical Palermo tips
- Italian-Americans reconnecting with Sicilian roots
- Digital nomads and expats considering Palermo as a base

---

## 4. Unique Selling Proposition

Nico is the USP. No other Palermo travel site has a personality like his. The content sounds like a person, not an algorithm. Travelers trust him because he is honest about the bad as well as the good.

What makes WAP different:
- Local expertise: everything comes from someone who lives and breathes the city
- Unfiltered style: no tourist fluff, no sponsored puff pieces
- Stand-up comedian tone: informative and entertaining at the same time
- Personal anecdotes woven into every piece of content

---

## 5. Current State of the Business (April 2026)

Revenue: approximately $20,000/year. All organic. Zero paid traffic. Zero strategy. This was achieved without a plan — purely through SEO and affiliate links.

Content:
- 79 blog posts on WordPress
- 35 pages on WordPress
- ~60 YouTube videos
- Last content published: approximately 4 months ago

Traffic source: almost entirely Google organic search. This is a risk — Google SEO is weakening and AI search is growing. Strategy needs to evolve.

## 9. Strategic Direction (2026)

The immediate priority is building the WAP Content Machine: an AI-assisted system for updating existing blog posts and creating new ones, running at ~80% autopilot with Nico's approval on every final output.

Longer term:
- Migrate The Sicilian Way from Podia to Lovable
- Reactivate social media with AI-assisted short-form content
- Launch paid advertising to accelerate traffic growth
- Build YouTube content workflow with AI scripting support
