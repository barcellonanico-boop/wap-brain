## Where-to-Stay Posts

Posts about WHERE to stay in Palermo, WHICH area, WHICH neighborhood. Includes:
- Where-to-stay-Palermo (main pillar)
- Where-to-stay-in-[specific-area] (spokes)
- Best neighborhoods for [audience type]
- Best hotels in [area]
- Where to stay if you have [constraint] (with kids, with car, on a budget, etc.)

### Cold Reader Reality Check (mandatory)

Cold readers (US tourists who never heard of WAP, never been to Italy) need these questions answered for EVERY area covered. Skip any of these and the post fails the reader.

For each area in the post:

1. **Vibe.** What's the area like? Who's there?
2. **Self-selection.** Who is this area good for? Who is it bad for?
3. **Safety.** Real safety. Day and night.
4. **Walking size.** How long to walk across this area? (e.g., "1.5 km corner to corner, 20 minutes")
5. **Distance to highlights.** How far to major attractions?
6. **Noise level.** Day. Night. Specific.
7. **Cleanliness.** Real talk. Don't varnish.
8. **Airport connection.** How to get from the airport to this area?
9. **Restaurants and food.** Available? Good? Open year-round?
10. **Supermarkets.** Especially for apartment stays. Real grocery stores or just alimentari?
11. **Night vibe.** Family-friendly? Lively? Dead?

### Star Rating Conventions

Mixing official Giata stars with editorial stars on apartments misleads readers. Implies authority that doesn't exist. Trust eroded when reader clicks an affiliate and the listing shows fewer stars than the post promised.

#### When to use ★ stars:

ONLY on properties officially classified by Italian government tourism authorities (Giata classification).

#### Properties that GET ★ stars:

- Hotels with formal classification (e.g., Hotel Wagner ★★★★★, Hotel Politeama ★★★★)
- Boutique hotels with formal classification (Palazzo Natoli ★★★★)
- B&Bs with formal classification (B&B L'Officina di Apollo ★★★)

#### Properties that DO NOT GET ★ stars:

- Apartments and vacation rentals (not officially classified)
- Airbnb listings (not officially classified)
- B&Bs without formal classification

#### What to use instead of stars:

A descriptive tagline only.

**Correct:**
> Politeama Apartments – (Apartments)
> Modern, clean, stylish | Excellent location

**Wrong:**
> Politeama Apartments – (Apartments)
> ★★★★ Modern, clean, stylish | Excellent location

#### How to verify:

When stating star ratings, verify against the hotel's own official site. Booking.com aggregator stars are sometimes wrong. The hotel's own site is canonical.

### Hotel Card Order Logic

In a where-to-stay post:

1. **Lead with Nico's top pick** for the area (best fit for the reader profile)
2. **Then alternates** in descending order of fit
3. **Luxury options** in a separate sub-section after main picks
4. **Nico's own properties** transparently labeled, NOT hidden as random Airbnb listings

### Local Truth Locks for Palermo

These local-truth facts are non-negotiable. Don't write contrary to them.

#### Centro Storico is ONE area, not 4 sub-neighborhoods

Locals call the entire historic center "Centro Storico." Tourist guides break it into Kalsa, Capo, Albergheria, Vucciria. Locals don't.

For accommodation purposes, treat Centro Storico as one unified area. Mention the four sub-neighborhood names for SEO capture, but DON'T treat them as separate stay options. Frame: "Tourist guides break it into four neighborhoods. We don't. Pick a hotel anywhere inside Centro Storico, you're fine."

#### Vucciria is currently a restaurant/nightlife area

NOT a working morning fish market. The "PATATE PATATE at 6 AM" story applies to Capo and Ballarò, NOT Vucciria.

Vucciria is touristy now, with restaurants and nightlife. Loud at night but not a market that opens at dawn.

If a draft frames Vucciria as a working morning market, reject and rewrite.

#### Capo and Ballarò are the chaotic working markets

Vendors shouting from dawn. Pickpocket risk during the day. Different chaos at night.

Don't book a hotel INSIDE Capo or Ballarò unless you want a 7 AM alarm clock of vendors yelling.

### Internal Linking Specifics for Where-to-Stay Posts

#### MUST link to:
- /districts/ (neighborhood overview hub)
- /news/airport-transfers/ (airport connection per area)
- /news/vehicle-parking-palermo/ (ZTL warning)
- /news/renting-car-palermo/ (for "with a car" mentions)
- /news/precautions-female-travelers-in-sicily/ (solo female section)

#### MUST NOT link to (Free vs Paid Conflict):
- /free-self-guided-walking-tours/ and any sub-paths
- /free-itinerary/

These free assets cannibalize The Sicilian Way premium guide. See WAP_03 for canonical rule.

### Word Count Tier

Where-to-stay posts are typically Standard Guide tier (1,500-2,500 words) or Comprehensive Guide tier (2,500-4,000 words) depending on number of areas covered.

For a 3-area where-to-stay post: target 3,000-3,500 words.

### Mandatory Sections in This Order

1. Italic lead
2. Featured image
3. Author intro (per WAP_06)
4. Top affiliate disclosure (per WAP_06)
5. TL;DR callout listing the areas with use-case mapping (per WAP_06)
6. Optional: short authority hook reinforcement
7. Per area, in order:
   - H2 with area name and tagline
   - 1-paragraph vibe intro
   - PROS / CONS / IDEAL FOR tables (canonical templates)
   - H3: Practical stuff (size, food, getting in) — covers Cold Reader Check items
   - H3: Why stay here
   - H3: Safety in [area]
   - H3: Best places to stay in [area] (hotel cards)
   - H3: Luxury option(s) in [area] (if any)
   - H3: Heads up / Where NOT to stay (if applicable)
8. FAQ section (6 questions, FAQPage schema)
9. Bottom line / recap
10. Bottom affiliate disclosure
11. Continue Planning block

### Where-to-Stay-Specific Audit Checklist

When SOP_01 runs on a where-to-stay post, in addition to WAP_06 generic audit:

- [ ] All Cold Reader Check items addressed for each area
- [ ] Star ratings verified against each hotel's own official site
- [ ] No editorial stars on apartments or vacation rentals
- [ ] Centro Storico framed as ONE area, not 4 sub-neighborhoods
- [ ] Vucciria fact lock respected (not framed as morning fish market)
- [ ] No links to free walking tours or free itineraries
- [ ] All 16 WAP_12 hotels covered (or explicit reason if any omitted)
- [ ] Hotel order: Nico's top pick first, alternates after, luxury separately
- [ ] Nico's own properties transparently labeled
