Last updated: April 19, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Total revenue: approximately $20,000/year as of April 2026. All organic. Zero paid traffic. Zero active strategy. This was achieved passively through SEO and affiliate links embedded in existing content.

Primary revenue driver: Booking.com affiliate links.
Secondary: Discover Cars affiliate links.
Minor: GetYourGuide affiliate links.
Product: The Sicilian Way premium guide.

---

## 2. Affiliate Revenue

### Booking.com
The main money-maker. Affiliate links are embedded across multiple pages, primarily:
- Where to Stay page (main traffic driver)
- Cefalu page
- San Vito Lo Capo page
- Other location-specific pages

How it works: visitor clicks a Booking.com link, makes a hotel reservation, Nico earns a commission.

### Discover Cars
Car rental affiliate program. Links embedded in relevant content (renting a car in Sicily, driving in Palermo, etc.).

### GetYourGuide
Experiences and tours affiliate program. Minor revenue. Links embedded in things-to-do content.

---

## 3. The Sicilian Way — Premium Guide

**What it is:** A premium travel guide to Sicily. Exclusive itineraries, top restaurant picks, practical insider advice not available for free on the blog.

**Platform:** Currently on Podia. Planned migration to Lovable in a future project.

**Pricing:**
- Full price: $47
- Downsell: $31 (offered after the soap opera sequence)

**Funnel:**

Step 1 — Optin: visitor lands on the free itinerary page on WordPress. Enters email to get a free Palermo itinerary PDF.

Step 2 — Soap Opera Sequence: ConvertKit email sequence. Tips and storytelling in Nico's voice. Builds trust and relationship before the pitch.

Step 3 — Pitch: after the sequence, subscriber is offered The Sicilian Way at $31 (downsell from $47).

Step 4 — Purchase: checkout on Podia. Delivery via Podia.

**Traffic source:** Almost entirely Google organic. Visitors find blog content, opt into the free itinerary, enter the funnel.

**Status:** Active and converting as of April 2026.
