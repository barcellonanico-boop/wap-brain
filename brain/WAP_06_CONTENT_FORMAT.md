# WAP_06_CONTENT_FORMAT.md — Content Format & Publishing Rules

## Hard Rules for Free Content

These rules apply to ALL free content: blog posts, YouTube videos, social media.

- NEVER mention specific restaurants or bars by name
- NEVER mention specific tour operators or experience providers by name
- Specific recommendations are exclusively for paid products (The Sicilian Way Guide, The Restaurant Guide)
- You can reference food categories and general areas (street food at the Ballarò market, seafood in the historic center) but never specific venues
